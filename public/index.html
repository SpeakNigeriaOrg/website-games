Experimental, and free to try

Learn Nigerian languages through play.

These are small, experimental games we built for our own students to practice with - real native-speaker audio, real words. Since they might be useful to other learners too, we're sharing them here, starting with Yoruba.

Available now

Syllable Builder

Build the word, syllable by syllable

Listen to a word spoken by a real Yoruba speaker, then tap its syllables in order - sorted by tone, with a hint if you need it. Free to play, no sign-up.
